L0ve2Watch

An attempt to articulate the feeling of falling too deep into someone online. The longing for their longing.
‍
The need to be seen, assured and understood. A craving for understanding, and admiration.
‍
To watch and be seen. When you can't look away because of love, curiosity and boredom.
‍
LOVE 2 WATCH is an intimate archive of my experience with watching and wanted to be watched. Through a series of screenshots, livestreams, memes, notes and messages. I will attempted to understand my feelings of Limerence online.
